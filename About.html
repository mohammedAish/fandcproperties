Why F&C Properties?

Our philosophy goes far beyond the Buy Property and Rent Property business model.
F&C Properties is a customer satisfaction-driven international team that continues to excel as the leading advocate of long-term relations with those who call Dubai “Home”. We believe that deep appreciation and mutual loyalty are what we offer to those who trust us.

F&C Properties is a one-stop-shop solution for Property Management, Buy Property, and Rent Property in Real Estate Dubai's growing market.

Enjoy comprehensive, high-quality, multi-lingual services based on the professional expertise that has been carefully built, stiving to the excellence of Dubai’s energy and spirit.

The professional and ethical qualities of our team gain trust among our happy customers that enjoy smooth processing of complicated Buy Property, Rent Property, and Property Management transactions.

As the founder of F&C Properties, I believe that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.
When it came to choosing a name for the business of my heart, I was offered different, long, complicated, and most of the time - meaningless company names. Based on a belief that long-term success comes only with honesty and responsibility, I have chosen the simple “F&C” as the acronym for “Farrukh and Company”, which reflects the founder’s commitment and responsibility with my name before the customers and company team.

We started small with a tiny business center desk space and steadily grew into a successful real estate agency that has been awarded as PropertyFinder’s “Rising Star” among over 1,300 Brokerage companies in the UAE.
